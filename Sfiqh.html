What Is a Fiqh ?

FIQH means Jurisprudence. It means the rules and orders of Shariah (Islam)on certain things based on the Imaams and .School of thoughts. In Islam there are only 4 School of Thoughts i.e (Madh-habs) مذاهب . Out of these 4 It is an order by the Prohpet to only follow 1 of these as preffered. All of these 4 Imaams and their thoughts (مذهب) are TRUE and therefore cannot be denied or refuted

Shaafi'i Madh-hab is one of them that is based on Imaam Muhammadush Shaafi'i (الإمام محمد الشافعي) school of thought (فقه). Followers of this imaam are reffered to as Shaafi'i Madh-hab (مذهب الشافعي). This school of thought / Jurisprudence came into existence from Imaam Shaafi'i (رضي الله عنه) . Imaam Shaafi'i (رضي الله عنه) School of thought was the 3rd School of thought out of the 4 Schools / Jurisprudence.
