This page is designed for fiqh (Jurisprudence ) purpose consisting of both Hanfi and Shafi Fiqh content.
Providing comprehensive guidance on Islamic jurisprudence, practices and principal for followers of Imaam Abu Haneefa and Imaam Muhammad Ibn Idrees Al-Shafi'i ( رضي الله عنهما )

This page has two parts. First part consisting of all the Shafi Fiqh while the second consist of Hanafi Fiqh .
