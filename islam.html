What Is Islam ?

اِنَّ الدِّيْنَ عِنْدَ اللهِ الإِسْلاَمْ

Translation: "Indeed the religion in the sight of Allah is Islam".
"بیشک اللہ کے نزدیک دین اسلام ہے "

(Quoted from Surah آل عمران)

Islam is one of the religions in the world that is based on the revealations of Allah ( جل جلاله) and the teachings of the holy book Quraan and Prophet Muhammad ( صلي الله عليه وسلم) Islam is the first religion that was revealed in the world then followed by the other religions.
Islam is religion that means "Peace and Obedience to Allah". Islam teaches monotheism, prayer, charity, fasting, justice and community among other principles.Islam is a religion that promotes peace, unity and spirituality among its followers.
Allah ( جل جلاله) has promised for those who believe in Allah, do deeds of righteousness,follow the way of Islam that for them there is forgiveness and a great reward (i.e. Paradise).
